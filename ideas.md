# Brainstorming: SKU Wood Tracker Design Ideas

## User Requirements
- **Core Systems:** SKU Development Tracker + Wood Cost Calculator.
- **Style:** Gamified & Modern (Colorful, Dimensional, Fun like a game).
- **Goal:** Track product development and calculate wood costs.
- **Key Features:** XP, Daily Challenges, Progress Bars, Dashboard.

<text>
<idea>
  <design_movement>Neo-Brutalism Pop</design_movement>
  <core_principles>
    1. **High Contrast & Boldness:** Unapologetic use of black outlines and vibrant, clashing colors to create energy.
    2. **Raw & Playful:** UI elements that look like stickers or physical game pieces.
    3. **Clarity through Exaggeration:** Large typography and oversized buttons to make interactions feel substantial.
    4. **Gamified Feedback:** Instant visual feedback (shakes, confetti, bold borders) for every action.
  </core_principles>
  <color_philosophy>
    **"Arcade Energy"**: A palette inspired by 90s arcade cabinets.
    - Primary: Electric Blue (#2563EB) & Hot Pink (#DB2777) for actions.
    - Background: Stark White or very light grid pattern.
    - Accents: Bright Yellow (#FACC15) for highlights/XP.
    - Text: Pitch Black (#000000) for maximum readability.
    - *Intent:* To keep the user awake and excited, treating work like a high-score run.
  </color_philosophy>
  <layout_paradigm>
    **"The Dashboard Console"**:
    - Asymmetric, card-based layout where containers have thick, hard shadows (no blur).
    - Sidebar looks like a game inventory menu.
    - Calculator looks like a retro handheld device.
  </layout_paradigm>
  <signature_elements>
    1. **Hard Shadows:** Buttons and cards have solid black drop shadows at 45 degrees.
    2. **Sticker Icons:** Icons that look like die-cut stickers with white borders.
    3. **Pixel/Glitch Accents:** Subtle pixel art decorations or glitch effects on hover.
  </signature_elements>
  <interaction_philosophy>
    **"Tactile Crunch"**: Buttons depress deeply when clicked. Inputs feel "chunky". Completing a task feels like smashing a "GO" button.
  </interaction_philosophy>
  <animation>
    - **Snappy & Bouncy:** Elements pop in with an elastic bounce.
    - **Hover States:** Elements shift position (translate) rather than just changing color.
    - **Progress Bars:** Fill up with a retro "loading" animation or blocks.
  </animation>
  <typography_system>
    - **Headings:** 'Chakra Petch' or 'Rubik Mono One' (Square, techy, bold).
    - **Body:** 'Space Grotesk' or 'JetBrains Mono' (Readable but with character).
  </typography_system>
</idea>
</text>

  <design_movement>Soft 3D Claymorphism (Nu-Clay)</design_movement>
  <core_principles>
    1. **Friendly & Approachable:** Soft, rounded shapes that look like they can be squished.
    2. **Depth without Heaviness:** Using inner shadows and soft gradients to create volume (3D feel) without harsh lines.
    3. **Pastel & Dreamy:** A soothing environment that makes work feel like play in a sandbox.
    4. **Fluid Gamification:** XP and progress flow like liquid or soft jelly.
  </core_principles>
  <color_philosophy>
    **"Candy Shop"**:
    - Primary: Bubblegum Pink (#F472B6) & Sky Blue (#60A5FA).
    - Background: Very soft Off-White or Cream (#FAFAF9).
    - Accents: Mint Green (#34D399) for success/profit.
    - *Intent:* To reduce stress and make the complex calculation/tracking process feel light and manageable.
  </color_philosophy>
  <layout_paradigm>
    **"Floating Islands"**:
    - Content lives on floating, pill-shaped containers.
    - No sharp corners; everything is `rounded-3xl`.
    - Calculator inputs look like soft indentations in the surface.
  </layout_paradigm>
  <signature_elements>
    1. **Inflated Elements:** Buttons and cards look like inflated balloons or clay.
    2. **Soft Gradients:** Subtle linear gradients on surfaces to suggest curvature.
    3. **Floating Avatars:** 3D-rendered style avatars or icons for the gamification elements.
  </signature_elements>
  <interaction_philosophy>
    **"Squishy Response"**: Buttons scale down slightly (squish) on click. Toggles slide with a heavy, satisfying friction.
  </interaction_philosophy>
  <animation>
    - **Floaty:** Idle elements might bob gently.
    - **Morphing:** Progress bars expand smoothly like filling a balloon.
    - **Transitions:** Soft fades and slides, nothing abrupt.
  </animation>
  <typography_system>
    - **Headings:** 'Fredoka' or 'Varela Round' (Rounded, friendly).
    - **Body:** 'Nunito' or 'Quicksand' (Rounded terminals, high legibility).
  </typography_system>

<idea>
  <design_movement>Cyber-Fantasy RPG</design_movement>
  <core_principles>
    1. **Immersive Interface:** The UI mimics a futuristic fantasy game HUD (Heads-Up Display).
    2. **Data as Power:** Numbers (Cost, Margin) are presented as stats (STR, INT).
    3. **Dark & Glowing:** Dark mode base with neon glows to highlight critical info.
    4. **Quest-Based Workflow:** Tasks are "Quests", Projects are "Campaigns".
  </core_principles>
  <color_philosophy>
    **"Mana & Gold"**:
    - Primary: Deep Purple (#4C1D95) background.
    - Accents: Neon Cyan (#22D3EE) for data, Gold (#FBBF24) for profit/XP.
    - Borders: Glowing semi-transparent borders.
    - *Intent:* To make the user feel like a high-level player managing their inventory and stats.
  </color_philosophy>
  <layout_paradigm>
    **"The Cockpit"**:
    - Fixed sidebar and header framing the content area.
    - Glassmorphism panels for data tables.
    - Hexagonal shapes for profile/level indicators.
  </layout_paradigm>
  <signature_elements>
    1. **Glass Panels:** Translucent backgrounds with background blur.
    2. **Hexagons:** Used for icons, levels, and progress nodes.
    3. **Glowing Text:** Key metrics have a subtle outer glow.
  </signature_elements>
  <interaction_philosophy>
    **"Magical Activation"**: Hovering triggers glows. Clicking emits a particle burst effect (visualized via CSS/SVG).
  </interaction_philosophy>
  <animation>
    - **Tech Reveal:** Lines drawing themselves before content appears.
    - **Pulse:** Critical alerts or "Daily Challenges" pulse gently.
    - **Level Up:** Full-screen overlay or significant visual flair when milestones are reached.
  </animation>
  <typography_system>
    - **Headings:** 'Orbitron' or 'Rajdhani' (Squared, futuristic).
    - **Body:** 'Exo 2' or 'Barlow' (Tech-forward but readable).
  </typography_system>
